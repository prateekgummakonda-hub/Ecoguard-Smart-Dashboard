Appliance Resource Consumption

  • Air Conditioner – High electricity demand, especially afternoon use
  • Water Heater – Moderate to high power usage during morning hours
  • Lighting – Low to moderate usage depending on time of day
  • Refrigerator – Continuous background consumption
  • Kitchen Appliances – Short bursts of energy use throughout the day

Steps to Reduce Consumption

  1. Reduce air conditioner runtime during peak heat hours by setting a stable temperature.
  2. Use the water heater only when necessary instead of leaving it active continuously.
  3. Switch off lights in unoccupied rooms and rely on daylight where possible.
  4. Avoid opening the refrigerator repeatedly to reduce cooling loss.
  5. Turn off standby appliances that continue to draw power when not in use.
