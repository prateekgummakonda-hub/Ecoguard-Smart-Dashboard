Design Summary Report

Use this section to explain your final product in your portfolio.

EcoGuard is a smart touchscreen enclosure designed to protect internal electronics while providing a premium user interface. The top opening is dedicated to a touchscreen display, the side vents improve cooling and airflow, and the front port supports charging and data transfer. Filleted edges improve safety and aesthetics, creating a smoother and more realistic consumer product design.
